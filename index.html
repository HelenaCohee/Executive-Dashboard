Executive Summary

Q1 was the highest-performing quarter in program history. We onboarded 487 new hires (up 12% from Q4) while simultaneously improving every quality metric. NPS jumped 8 points to 72, completion rate hit 94%, and time-to-productivity dropped by 2 full days.

Three things drove this: (1) the self-guided pre-boarding toolkit launched in January cut Day 1 confusion significantly, (2) we restructured the facilitator rotation to match expertise to audience, and (3) the AI-powered intake system reduced support ticket resolution from 48 hours to 6 hours for onboarding-related issues.

The main risk going into Q2 is venue capacity — our current space maxes at 120 per cohort, and we're projecting 550+ new hires. Venue strategy is in the "Risks & Decisions" section below.

Strategic Insights
What Worked
Pre-boarding Toolkit
Self-guided digital toolkit reduced Day 1 "information overload" feedback by 40%. New hires arrived with context, not confusion. Expanding to all departments in Q2.
Watch Closely
Remote Cohort Experience
Remote-only participants rated the experience 12 points lower on NPS. Investigating whether this is content, delivery, or connection gap. Piloting hybrid model in Q2.
Opportunity
AI Support Triage
AI-powered intake system resolved 68% of onboarding support tickets without human intervention. Expanding scope to cover benefits enrollment and systems access questions.
